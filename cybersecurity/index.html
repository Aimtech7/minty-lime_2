Our Security Assessment Process

1

Discovery & Scoping

We begin by understanding your organization's security needs, identifying assets, and defining the scope of the assessment.

2

Vulnerability Identification

Using automated tools and manual techniques, we identify security vulnerabilities across your systems and applications.

3

Exploitation & Validation

We validate identified vulnerabilities through controlled exploitation to understand their real-world impact.

4

Reporting & Remediation

We deliver comprehensive reports with prioritized recommendations and work with your team to implement fixes.

5

Re-testing & Verification

We conduct follow-up assessments to verify that vulnerabilities have been properly remediated.
